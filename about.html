Our Story

Why we do what we do

With over 20 years of experience selling tacos in Mexico the Torres family decided to start their own Taco Stand in Peoria, Arizona. Here at Tacos Tijuana we have a passion to serve our community. Each of our tacos are made with a choice of Carne Asada or Al pastor and made with fresh handmade tortillas. We strive to give each of our customers a quality experience and a taste of great authentic mexican tacos.
